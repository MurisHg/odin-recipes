Hurmashice

Description

A traditional beloved recipe.Beloved in southeastern Eu. Simple yet tasty.Soft texture and very sweet

Ingredients

  • 1/3 puter
  • 1/3 sugar
  • 1/3 flour
  • 3eggs
  • Additional sugar for water+sugar that gets poured over desert

Preparation

Mix ingredients. Result should be little bit too sticky to make cookies out of. And thats ok. Put in freezer for 30 min or so. That should make it managable to shape. We do this because this way cookie will be way softer and will take in more suguar water. Once cooked. Cook, once done while cookies are still hot pour water mixed with sugar that you prepared.
